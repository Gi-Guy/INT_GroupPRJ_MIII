Push-Up Workout

Push-ups are one of the most effective bodyweight exercises for strengthening the chest, shoulders, and triceps, while also engaging the core and stabilizing muscles. This workout is a great way to build upper body strength and endurance.

To perform a push-up, start in a high plank position with your hands shoulder-width apart. Lower your body towards the ground while keeping your core tight, then push back up to the starting position. Aim for 3 sets of 10-15 reps depending on your fitness level.

Incorporating push-ups into your workout routine can help improve your overall strength and fitness, as well as enhance functional movements in daily life.
