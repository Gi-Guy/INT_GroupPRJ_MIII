Squat Workout

Squats are a fundamental lower-body exercise that targets the quadriceps, hamstrings, and glutes while also engaging the core. They are great for building strength and improving mobility, and they can be done with or without additional weights.

To perform a squat, stand with your feet shoulder-width apart and your toes pointing slightly outward. Lower your body as if you are sitting down, keeping your knees behind your toes and your chest lifted. Push through your heels to return to the starting position. Aim for 3 sets of 12-15 reps.

Including squats in your workout routine can enhance leg strength, improve posture, and help with functional movements in daily life.
