Lunges Workout

Lunges are a highly effective lower-body exercise that targets the quads, hamstrings, glutes, and calves. This exercise helps strengthen and tone the legs while also improving balance and stability.

To perform a basic lunge, start by standing with your feet together. Take a large step forward with one leg, bending both knees to lower your body toward the ground, making sure your front knee stays behind your toes. Push off with your front leg to return to the starting position. Alternate legs and repeat for 10-12 reps per leg.

Lunges are great for building leg strength, improving mobility, and increasing functional movement, making them a staple in any fitness routine.
