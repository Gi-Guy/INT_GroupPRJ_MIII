Plank Workout

The plank is a powerful core exercise that engages multiple muscle groups, focusing on the abdominals, back, shoulders, and glutes. It is an excellent move for building core strength, stability, and improving posture.

To perform a basic plank, begin in a forearm plank position with your elbows under your shoulders and your body in a straight line from head to heels. Keep your core tight and hold for as long as you can, aiming for 30 seconds to 1 minute for beginners.

Planks can be modified by adding leg lifts, side planks, or by increasing the duration for more advanced practitioners. This exercise is a great addition to any fitness routine for toning and strengthening the entire core.
