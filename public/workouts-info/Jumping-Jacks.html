Jumping Jacks Workout

Jumping jacks are a simple yet highly effective full-body exercise that improves cardiovascular health, coordination, and endurance. They involve jumping to a position with your legs spread wide and your hands touching overhead, then returning to a standing position with your feet together.

To perform jumping jacks, start by standing with your feet together and your arms by your sides. Jump up, spreading your legs while raising your arms overhead. Jump again to return to the starting position. Repeat for 30 seconds to 1 minute, depending on your fitness level.

Jumping jacks are great for warming up, improving endurance, and engaging the entire body in a fun and effective way.

~ 100-150 Calories Burned
