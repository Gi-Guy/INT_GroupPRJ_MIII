High Knees Workout

High knees are an excellent cardiovascular exercise that also helps tone the lower body. This exercise primarily targets the hip flexors, quadriceps, and calves while also providing a great cardio workout.

To perform high knees, stand with your feet hip-width apart. Begin running in place by lifting your knees as high as possible toward your chest. Try to keep a quick pace and use your arms for momentum. Aim for 30 seconds to 1 minute of high knees, and repeat for 3-4 sets depending on your fitness level.

High knees are great for improving cardiovascular endurance, boosting calorie burn, and enhancing lower body strength and coordination.

~ 100-150 Calories Burned
