Burpees Workout

Burpees are a full-body exercise that combines a squat, a jump, and a push-up into one dynamic movement. They are known for being an intense, calorie-burning workout that builds strength, agility, and cardiovascular endurance.

To perform a burpee, start standing with your feet shoulder-width apart. Drop into a squat position, place your hands on the floor, kick your feet back into a plank position, do a push-up, then quickly reverse the movement by jumping your feet forward and explosively jumping into the air. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.

Burpees are great for building functional fitness and improving cardiovascular endurance. While they are challenging, they are also highly effective in burning fat and strengthening various muscle groups.

~ 10-15 Calories Burned per Minute
